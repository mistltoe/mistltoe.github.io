Basic protocol
1. Image source: tiff files exported from mrxs(3DHISTECH Ltd.) by ParanormicViewer
2. Image Scale: large(5000 x 5000 in femur image or 4000 x 12000 in calvaria), resize(25% x 25%)
3. Colour deconvolution: H&E -> Colour_2 for cytosol area (hematoxylin stain)
4. Calculation of stained Area = (Area*pArea)/(sArea*spArea*10000)
